Family, Cosmetic & Implant Dentists Located in Brooklyn, NY

Quentin Smile Dental is a modern, family-owned dental practice located in the heart of Brooklyn, NY. We’ve been proudly serving our community for over 25 years when the office started out in a small location on Quentin Rd. In 2016, the father-son duo of Dr.’s Igor and Alexander Khabensky expanded the practice to its new facility on Ocean Avenue.

Today, the dental practice is a state-of-the-art facility which features many advanced technologies from dental lasers to low radiation 3D scanning to 3D printing and intra-oral scanning. That means an approach that focuses on comprehensive treatment, thorough planning, and the highest precision and accuracy of dental treatment

SPECIALIZING IN DENTAL IMPLANT PROCEDURES

We offer the latest in cosmetic dental and functional replacement for missing teeth: dental implants. Implants require dental surgery, but they become embedded teeth replacements — unlike dentures and dental bridges you don’t have to remove them to clean them.
